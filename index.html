Welcome to Spectra Corps!

We are a community focused around building a safe space for friendly and chill gaming, whatever you like to play! (Especially Destiny though :3) We love bringing in new players and helping them get deeper and more skilled with all sorts of games, as well as bolstering new friendships and experiences through community playdates and activities. We even have a burgeoning creative space for people creating in all sorts of mediums to build their skills, show off their work, and collaborate. If you're queer of some manner, friendly, and looking for like-minded folks, we'd love to have you!
